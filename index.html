Hi, I'm Yumeng.

I was born and raised in Beijing. I studied computing science in college and economics in graduate school, only to learn that I wasn't meant to live inside equations or models alone. What has always pulled me forward is something quieter and deeper: how people think, and how technology subtly reshapes the way we see and move through the world.

I began my career in product management, where I learned to listen—to data, to users, to what isn't said. I ran experiments, tested assumptions, and built products that, in small but meaningful ways, made life a little easier or better for the users.

Today, I work with AI. Not because it is new or impressive, but because I believe it will profoundly change how we create, decide, and imagine the future—and I want to be part of shaping that change with care and intention.

Beyond work, I'm drawn to nature and movement: swimming, skiing, rock climbing, tennis. Spiritually, I remain endlessly curious—about consciousness, creativity, and the unknown—continuing an inward journey alongside the outward one.

I love to create. I stay curious. And I'm still becoming.
